XResearch

XResearch is a self-funded AI research lab focused on AGI.

We believe that a general-purpose intelligent system akin to the Wright brothers first airplane has been achieved, and our research is tailored towards the development of stronger versions of artificial super intelligence.

The research roadmap will be curiosity-driven, focused on problems at the intersection of low-resource high-impact.

Basically, we're focused on questions that can be explored on a personal computer, or tiny cluster.
